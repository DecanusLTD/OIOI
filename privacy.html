Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

OIOI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the OIOI mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account details (such as name, email address, and username)
  • Challenge participation data (goals, progress, submissions, forfeits)
  • Uploaded proof content (such as photos or text submissions)
  • Usage data (app interactions, feature usage, diagnostics)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and operate the OIOI app
  • Enable group challenges and shared accountability
  • Track progress, forfeits, and shared saving pots
  • Send reminders and notifications for daily engagement
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Payments & Money Handling

OIOI does not process payments directly. Any forfeits, transfers, or shared payments are agreed upon privately between group members using external payment methods. OIOI is not a bank, payment processor, or escrow service.

Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with members of the groups you choose to join. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

Children

OIOI is not intended for users under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
